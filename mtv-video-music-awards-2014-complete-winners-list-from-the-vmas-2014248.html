MTV Video Music Awards 2014: Complete Winners List From the VMAs

 Posted on April 4, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  646 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

They're back! After a headline-making 2013 awards show complete with twerking and foam fingers, music's biggest stars hit Inglewood, Calif. on Sunday, Aug. 24, for MTV's 2014 Video Music Awards. 

The VMAs kicked off with Beyonce leading the night with eight nominations, including nods for Video of the Year for "Drunk in Love" with her husband Jay Z, Best Female Video for "Partition," and Best Video With a Social Message for "Pretty Hurts." She was also one of the top-billed performers. 

Other leading nominees included Eminem, Iggy Azalea, and Katy Perry.

Check out all of the winners below and check back throughout the night as the list is updated!
