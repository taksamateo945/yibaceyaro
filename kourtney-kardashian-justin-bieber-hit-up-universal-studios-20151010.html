Kourtney Kardashian and Justin Bieber Hit Up Universal Studios

 Posted on April 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  293 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Friday night fun! Kourtney Kardashian and Justin Bieber hung out together on Friday, Oct. 9, partying at a West Hollywood club and hitting Universal Studios.

The “What Do You Mean?” singer, 21, and the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star, 36, partied at The Nice Guy in L.A, where they also ate dinner and were seen leaving together in the same car.

Kardashian looked casual in a tan top with white pants, while Bieber wore a camouflage jacket and a gray hat.

An eyewitness said that at one point, the singer played a pop-up performance on the piano at Nice Guy, while Kardashian laughed and smiled with her friends.

And on Saturday, Oct. 10, Selena Gomez’s ex-boyfriend shared a photo on Instagram that showed him and the mom of three wearing 3-D glasses at Universal Studios on Friday night, for its “Halloween Horror Nights.” Fans also tweeted about seeing the pair at the amusement park on Friday. “Mm ok that’s random,” one tweeter wrote.

The pop star, who recently took a cute, goofy selfie with Kardashian’s half-sister Kendall Jenner, is a close friend of the Kardashian-Jenner family. In May, the Biebs even shared a photo on Instagram of him playing with Kardashian’s eldest son Mason, 5, in the pool.
