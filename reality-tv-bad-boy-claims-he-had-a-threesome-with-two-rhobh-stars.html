Reality TV bad boy claims he had a THREESOME with two RHOBH stars

 Posted on August 16, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  514 words  |  Martina Birk

A reality TV bad boy has set social media alight by claiming he once had a threesome with two stars of the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills. Garrett Morosky, who appeared in season one of FBOY Island and went on to become the series’ villain for choosing money over his relationship in the finale, made wild claims about a romp with two Bravo stars on a new podcast.

While appearing on Marco DelVecchio’s Smoochie Town raw and unfiltered podcast on Thursday, the 29-year-old TV personality claims he ‘smashed’ two women from the hit show. He didn’t reveal when the alleged encounter took place, nor did he reveal the identity of the women he claims were involved. DailyMail.com has reached out to Bravo for comment on the shock allegations. Without naming names, Morosky recalled shaking the hands of one of the women’s husbands after the alleged sexual encounter.

He claimed that he and the star had a ‘threesome’ with ‘one of her friends who’s also on the show midday… in her husband’s bed.’ ‘After that, I went in the back and was like, jumping on a trampoline with like her kid and stuff,’ he alleged. ‘Bro, it was one of the most ridiculous things.’ When the woman’s husband showed up, he said he asked her: ‘[Expletive] what do I do.’ He claims she said for him to say he’s a ‘real estate agent’ checking out the house. DelVecchio mused that Morosky’s acting classes must’ve really ‘paid off’ in that moment.

Later, Morosky says he got a text message from the husband saying he would ‘love to meet you’ in Beverly Hills to ‘chat with some business.’ At the dinner, Morosky claims the man came with a dude who looked like ‘a secret service’ and told him: ‘Listen, if you ever come around my family or my kids ever, again, my buddy, right here, will take care of you.’ 

After DelVecchio commented on Instagram that this was the ‘craziest story’ on his podcast yet, his pal described the women as ‘cougars preying on a young boy.’ The interview sparked a flood of online speculation over which Real Housewives he could possibly could have hooked up with.

Morosky is best known for starring on FBoy Island, which is a reality dating series on HBO Max, hosted Nikki Glaser. During the show, women are forced to choose between self-proclaimed ‘Nice Guys’ and ‘FBoys’ with $100,000 on the line. It is shot in the Cayman Islands and centered on three ladies that are tested on their ability to choose from a pool of 24 eligible men on which are looking for a serious relationship or just cash and casual [sexual intercourse].
