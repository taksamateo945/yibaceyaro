Elin Nordegren, Chris Cline Split After One Year of Dating

 Posted on April 10, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  202 words  |  Larita Shotwell

On to the next! Elin Nordegren and her billionaire boyfriend Chris Cline have split after one year of dating, a source reveals to Us Weekly.

The Swedish model, 34, and the 54-year-old coal tycoon began dating in March 2013, marking the first serious relationship for Nordegren since her split from golf champ Tiger Woods. (Nordegren and Woods, 38, finalized their divorce in 2010 following his monumental cheating scandals.)

As Us exclusively reported, Nordegren and Woods — who share daughter Sam, 6, and son Charlie, 5 — have remained amicable since the split. In fact, the exes have gone as far as going on double dates with Woods and girlfriend Lindsey Vonn.
