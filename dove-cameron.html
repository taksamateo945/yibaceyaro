Dove Cameron Biography

Chloe Celeste Hosterman, better known by her professional name Dove Cameron, was born in Seattle, Washington, on January 15, 1996. Cameron’s parents separated when she was a toddler. Claire, her elder sister, is her sibling. Cameron attended Burbank High School, which is located in Burbank, California.

She is best known for her appearances as Liv/Maddie Rooney in the Disney Channel comedy “Liv & Maddie.” In addition, the young actress played Kayla Morgan in the film Cloud 9 as well (the 2014 Disney Channel original film). Additionally, she is well-known for her roles in Descendants and Descendants 2. She has a penchant for singing as well as acting. Four soundtrack albums, eight singles, and thirteen music videos have been published by Dove.

Career

In 2007, she performed the part of young Cosette in Bainbridge Performing Arts’ production of The Secret Garden, and in 2008, she played the main role of Mary in the same production. Dove Cameron was cast in the 2012 Disney television series “Liv & Maddie.” She portrayed two distinct roles. Disney was compelled to revive this series in 2014 due to public demand for the program.

In 2013, she released her debut version of Imagine Dragons’ “On Top of the World,” which peaked at number 17 on Billboard. Later that year, she released her first single ‘Better in Stereo,’ which topped the Billboard charts and propelled her to prominence. Additionally, she published ‘Count Me In,’ ‘Rotten to the Core,’ and ‘If Only’ consecutively.

In 2016, Cameron participated in NBC Live’s “Hairspray Live!,” for which the crowd appreciated her performance. Due to her appearances in Disney Specials, she is now very famous among children, and every adolescent girl desires to be her or is inspired by her work.
