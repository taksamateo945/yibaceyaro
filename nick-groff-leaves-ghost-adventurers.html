Nick Groff Ghost Adventures Career

In the year 2004, Nick Groff collaborate with Aaron Goodwin and Zak Bagans to co-produce a documentary typed TV series known as Ghost Adventures. The paranormal show premiered on Travel Channel in October 2018. This program revolves around three paranormal ghost hunters, Aaron Goodwin, Zak Bagans and Nick Groff as they search the places and locations that have experienced paranormal hauntings.

Currently, the show is running successfully and is in its eighteen seasons with many other stars like Billy Tolley and Adam Goodin. Ghost Adventures went to become one of the successful paranormal reality TV shows in American Television history. The impressive show has won several multiple awards like Spooktalkular Parawards and International Paranormal Acknowledgement Awards.

Why did Nick Groff Leave Ghost Adventures?

In 2014, it came as a shock to many fans of Ghost Adventures that Nick Groff will not work any longer in the show. There were many speculations concerning his departure. He officially confirmed his exit from the show from his Facebook on 24 November 2014 thanking his well-wishers and supporters.

There were rumours that the Ghost Adventures star, Nick Groff was fired. However, they are no official concrete evidence to back this statement. Many experts assumed the reason he left Ghost Adventure was he was committing himself to a new show, Ghost Stalkers which premiered on 19 October 2014.

Moreover, his fellow co-producer, Zak Bagans, made many tweets hinting that Nick was getting fired from the show. Furthermore, Zack, in one of his tweets, ordered an unnamed man who is possibly presumed to be Nick to quit calling Ghost Adventures as his achievement and accomplishment.

Additionally, there was also reported that Nick Groff was fired from Ghost Adventures as a result of him breaching the contract. He was working simultaneously for both Ghost Stalkers and Ghost Adventures at the same time as executive producers.

The main reason for Nick’s exit from the show has not been officially disclosed. He has gone on to produce Paranormal Lockdown which premiered on TV in early March 2016.

Nick Groff New Show

After quitting the Ghost Adventures in 2014, Nick Groff produced went on to produce paranormal investigations show such as Ghost Stalkers and Paranormal Lockdown. Besides his role of executive producer and paranormal investigator in such shows. He has also hosted the Destination America show, Ghosts of Shepherdstown for a couple of seasons.

Moreover, Nick also worked as the executive producer for Travel Channel series, Vegas Stripped.
