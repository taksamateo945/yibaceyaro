Hailey Bieber Celebrates Her 1 Year Anniversary With Justin Bieber

 Posted on July 7, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  368 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

According to a report from Entertainment Tonight, it’s been approximately one year since Hailey and Justin got married, and the fashion model took to her social media to announce their very first anniversary together.

In her Instagram Stories, Hailey shared a few pictures of herself hanging around her husband, the “As Long As You Love Me” singer, including one of which featured her kissing the Purpose singer on the cheek while they’re standing in an elevator.

As it was previously reported, Hailey and Justin got married in a New York City courthouse in September of 2018, confirming what fans had expected for a while. Around two weeks later, insiders, who spoke with ET confirmed they were married.

Paraphrasing what the source explained at the time, Justin and Hailey were thrilled to get married and literally couldn’t wait, however, they wanted to keep it a big secret for the sake of privacy. Moreover, they still intend on having a more formal ceremony, but for now, things are good the way they are.

Reportedly, Justin and Hailey are actually getting ready to have their second wedding attended by family and friends later in September in the state of South Carolina. Sources who spoke with the aforementioned outlet stated they were “gearing up” for the most spectacular event of their lives.

As fans of the couple know, Hailey and Justin are both, incredibly religious, with Hailey, in particular, coming from a long line of relatively pious family members. Justin Bieber, on the other hand, turned to God and organized religion after his meteoric rise to fame as a teenage boy.

For years, Justin’s life and reputation were marred by incidents with the paparazzi, crazed fans, and lawsuits. However, after finding faith in God, the singer appeared to turn over a new leaf, moreover, his music, including Purpose, his last big album, began to reflect his religious convictions.
