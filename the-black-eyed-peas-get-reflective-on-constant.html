The Black Eyed Peas get reflective on 'Constant'

 Posted on April 24, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  194 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The Black Eyed Peas just keep delivering, and as described as member will.i.am below, their new single ‘Constant’ is a salute to the fans who have been there from the beginning.

The new single which is divided into two sections, features US rapper Slick Rick in the first part, over a hip hop beat reminiscent to their earlier style whilst the song switches to a more dance melody on the second part featuring Jessica Reynoso who is the current female vocalist of the group. ‘Constant’ follows in the footsteps of ‘Ring The Alarm’ and ‘Get It’ as the latest single from their upcoming project.

If you’re enjoying the trio’s new material as much as we are, then don’t forget you can catch them and Jessica live on The Masters Of The Sun UK & European tour which kicks off in October.
