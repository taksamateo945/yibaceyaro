Sabrina Carpenter Unveils Deluxe Album &quot;Emails I Cant Send Fwd&quot;

 Posted on April 28, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  869 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Critically acclaimed singer-songwriter Sabrina Carpenter unveiled an extended deluxe version of her 2022 studio album, titled “Emails I Can’t Send Fwd,” released on Friday, March 17, 2023, via Island Records/Universal Music Canada.

This latest album follows the release of her acclaimed fifth studio album, “Emails I Can’t Send” in 2022, spanning a multitude of hit singles such as “Skin,” “Skinny Dipping,” “Fast Times,” “Because I Loved A Boy,” “Vicious,” and “Nonsense.” The original release has 13 songs, and the deluxe edition comprises four additional previously unreleased songs for the album—”Opposite,” “Feather,” “Lonesome” and “Things I Wish You Said”—that build upon Sabrina’s most raw and vulnerable songwriting to date.

Although, the singer has cleared that the set of new songs is not a “follow up to anything,” [but] rather new tracks that belong to her current era.

After months of teasing and waiting, fans finally felt relieved when Sabrina announced the deluxe album release on March 9, 2023, via her social media. Writing on Instagram, she said: “For those of you that found a little piece of yourselves in this album, think of these few new songs as my sincerest thank you to you.”

She added: “So much happened in this chapter of my life that it was really hard to put it to rest, and ultimately I couldn’t stop writing and still can’t. It’s not a follow-up to anything, it’s just a few songs that belong in the ‘Emails’ world for those of you who were kind enough to make this record feel so welcome and heard.”

At the end of her post, Carpenter teased fans they might get to hear the new tracks in her current tour.

Sabrina Carpenter wears many hats as an artist and has enchanted an audience of millions through her enormous talent and hard-hitting social commentary. With her music, she has delivered one anthem after another on stage, singing to sold-out crowds and in the studio, with a growing musical catalogue encompassing multiple gold singles and acclaimed album releases. On-screen, she has generated mega-fandom through starring roles on television and film.
