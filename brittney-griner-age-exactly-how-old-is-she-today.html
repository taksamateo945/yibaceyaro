Brittney Griner is a 33-year-old female basketball player with the Women’s National Basketball Association in the United States. 

The WNBA player started doing exploits in basketball at a young age and today, she has made a name in the game both in the United States and abroad in such counties as Russia and China.

How Old is Brittney Griner Now?

Brittney Griner is currently 33 as she was born on the 18th of October 1990. She is the daughter of Harris County sheriff, Raymond Griner, and his wife, Sandra Griner. The star athlete has African-American roots, and owing to the fact that she was born in Houston, Texas, she is an American by nationality.

As young as she is, Brittany has recorded a lot of exceptional achievements that most people her cage can only dream of. Details on that below…

She Began Playing Varsity Basketball in Her Early Teens

Griner’s basketball career started blooming when she was still a high school student at Nimitz High School. She was quite exceptional at that level, practicing with the boys instead of the girls.

She took her experience to the collegiate level at Baylor University, Waco, Texas where she joined the Baylor Lady Bears. Griner proceeded to break set records with The National Collegiate Athletic Association scoring 2,000 points in addition to blocking 500 shots.

The female player swept uncountable titles during her days in both high school and college and has continued into her professional level.

She Was in Her Junior Season When Her Dunking Video Went Viral

Brittney was in her junior year in high school when her dunking video which surfaced on YouTube went viral. The said video accumulated over 6.6 million views within a short period of time attracting the attention of basketball player cum analyst, Shaquille O’Neal.

Brittney Griner Went Professional at the Age of 23

In 2013, Brittney Griner was drafted by the Phoenix Mercury as the first overall pick of the WNBA draft. She did exploits during her rookie year in the Women’s National Basketball Association, earning the title of WNBA all-star. In no time, she became a dominant force in the WNBA averaging 3.0 blocks per game.

Wearing the no. 42 jersey for the Phoenix Mercury team, Griner was named three-time All-American. In 2016, she joined the USA Olympic Women’s team, leading them to victory at the Summer Olympics winning her first gold. She was also part of the 2020 Tokyo Olympics representing America; she earned her second gold medal in the competition.

She Was Barley 21 When She Started Playing Overseas

During the WNBA off-season, Brittney Griner would occupy herself by playing professional basketball overseas. She has played in such countries as China where she inked a four-month contract to the tune of $600,000 with the Zhejiang Golden Bulls under the WCBA. This happened during the Off-season of 2013.

She has also been playing for the team UMMC Ekaterinburg in Russia since 2014 and has helped then win back-to-back championships.

At the Time Brittney Griner Got Arrested in Russia, She Was 32

While on her recurring trips to Russia for the off-season in February 2022, Brittney fell into the net of Russian customs for being in possession of cartridges containing below a gram of hashish oil. She pleaded guilty when her trial commenced on the 1st of July, earning a nine-year jail term on the 4th of August.

Griner Will Be 41 When she will be Released From Jail

On the 4th of August 2022, Griner went into jail as a 32–year old lady. She is expected to finish her nine-year sentence in 2031, meaning that by the time she will finally regain freedom, she will be 41 years old.
